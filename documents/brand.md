# Brand Notes

- Logo file: `public/brand/multazim_logo.png`
- Light surfaces: use on `bg-sand-50` or white
- Dark surfaces: use on `bg-ink-900`
- Clear space: keep at least 1x logo height around the mark
- Minimum size: 32px height for UI, 48px for headers
- Typeface pairing: Space Grotesk (display), IBM Plex Sans (body)
